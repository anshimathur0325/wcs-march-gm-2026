About Women in Computer Science at UIUC

Women in Computer Science (WCS) at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign is a student organization dedicated to supporting, mentoring, and promoting women in computing and technology fields. The organization creates a community where members can collaborate, learn, and develop both technical and professional skills.

Mission & Goals

WCS aims to increase representation of women in computing by providing mentorship, networking opportunities, workshops, and social events. Their programs encourage participation in hackathons, research projects, and professional development initiatives.

Activities & Events

  • Weekly meetings and social events to build community
  • Mentorship programs connecting students with peers and alumni
  • Workshops on coding, software development, and career skills
  • Participation in hackathons, tech competitions, and research projects
  • Networking with industry professionals through panels and talks

Impact

WCS at UIUC has helped hundreds of students gain confidence, improve technical skills, and connect with opportunities in technology careers. By fostering an inclusive environment, WCS encourages women to thrive in computing and leadership roles.
